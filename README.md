## About me

I come from an AI and data engineering background, previously working as an AI Engineer at ABN AMRO, where I built and deployed production-grade AI systems, including LLM, RAG and financial document automation solutions.

Today, I lead technology at [SKUU](https://www.skuu.io/), designing the systems, infrastructure and product foundations behind our retail inventory network.

- Building scalable retail technology at [SKUU](https://www.skuu.io/)
- Working across AI, backend systems, infrastructure and product engineering
- MSc in Computer Science — Artificial Intelligence, Leiden University
- Based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands
- Reach me at **luca@skuu.io**
- Occasionally flipping people on the mat 🥋

## Current focus

At SKUU, we are building a smarter retail inventory network that helps retailers recover lost sales from out-of-stock products by connecting fragmented stock across stores and partners.

My focus includes:

- System architecture and technical strategy
- Inventory integrations and data infrastructure
- Product availability and matching systems
- AI-driven retail intelligence
- Engineering operations, security and scalability
